Hiking

Mountains are the only place I find the same clarity I get from a well-designed system. The goal is obvious. The path is not. I've trekked the Himalayas, and I'm always planning the next one.

🎸

Guitar

I play often enough to improve slowly, poorly enough to stay humble. There's something in tracing why a chord progression doesn't resolve that feels a lot like debugging. I do it anyway.

📷

Photography

Mostly landscapes and light. I moved from phone shots to something more deliberate — paying attention to what a scene looks like before I frame it, rather than after.

🧭

Wandering

Buddhist temples in Mussoorie, rooftop sessions in the hills, random detours with no agenda. Some of my best ideas arrived in places I had no reason to be.
